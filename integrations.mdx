Attlas is designed to connect with the content, tools, and platforms you already use.

In this section, you'll find detailed guides for setting up and getting the most out of every available integration.

Integrations allow you to extend your chat's capabilities, import knowledge from different sources, and create even richer experiences for your users.

Here, you'll learn how to connect platforms, configure specific features, and use each integration effectively.

Choose an integration below to access its setup and usage guide.
