**Open Graph** allows you to control how your chat appears when shared on search engines, messaging apps and social media platforms.

It helps create a more professional presentation and increases the likelihood that people will click on your link.

<Check>
  To configure your chat's Open Graph settings, go to `Settings → Profile`and scroll down until you find the Open Graph section.
</Check>

## What is Open Graph?

Open Graph is a standard used by platforms such as:

- Google
- WhatsApp
- Facebook
- LinkedIn
- X (Twitter)
- Discord
- Telegram

When someone shares your chat link, these platforms use the information configured in Open Graph to generate the preview shown to users.

---

## Available settings

### SEO Title

The title that will be displayed when your chat appears in search results or link previews.

Examples:

- XYZ Company Help Center
- Complete Digital Marketing Guide

We recommend using short, clear and easy-to-identify titles.

### Meta description

The description displayed alongside the title.

Its purpose is to quickly explain what users will find when they access your chat.

Examples:

- Get answers about our products and services.
- Access content, links and resources in one place.

The clearer your description is, the more likely you are to attract qualified visitors.

---

## Why configure Open Graph?

Properly configuring these settings offers several benefits:

- A more professional appearance when sharing links;
- Better understanding of your chat's purpose;
- Increased trust from people who receive the link;
- Higher click-through rates on social media and messaging platforms.

Small improvements can make a significant difference in how your chat is perceived.

---

## Best Practices

### Use clear titles

- Avoid generic titles.
- Choose names that clearly identify your chat and its purpose.

### Keep descriptions short

- Briefly explain what users can expect when they access your chat.

### Think about first impressions

- In many cases, the Open Graph preview will be someone's first interaction with your Attlas chat.
- Make sure it accurately represents your brand, project, or content.

---

## Create more professional shares

Open Graph helps your chat make a strong first impression before the conversation even begins.

By properly configuring your title and description, you make your shared links clearer, more professional and more attractive to your audience.
