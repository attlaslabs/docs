## Why optimize your Attlas?

Many creators build a chat and stop there.

But the true power of Attlas appears when you start **customizing the chat experience**.

You can adjust:

- How your chat communicates
- How it starts conversations
- How it responds to questions
- How it presents itself visually

These adjustments can transform a simple chat into **a true assistant for your audience**.

---

## Choosing an archetype

To make personalization easier, Attlas offers **12 different archetypes**.

Each archetype comes with predefined settings that influence how the chat communicates.

Some archetypes are more:

- Educational
- Supportive
- Analytical
- Direct

You can choose the one that best matches your communication style.

Even after selecting an archetype, you can still customize its settings.

This allows you to create **more than 324 personality variations** for your chat.

The result is a chat that truly feels like **an extension of the way you communicate**.

## Encouraging conversations with triggers

After defining your chat's personality, the next step is encouraging users to start interacting.

Many people want to ask questions but don't know exactly **where to begin**.

This is where triggers become important.

---

## What are triggers?

Triggers are **buttons that help users start interactions with your chat**.

They allow visitors to begin a conversation with a single click.

These buttons can:

- Ask questions
- Open links
- Display content
- Redirect users to social media profiles

This reduces friction and significantly increases engagement.

---

## Available trigger types

Attlas supports several different trigger types.

### Questions

Buttons that automatically send a question to the chat.

Examples:

- "Where should I start?"
- "What tools do you recommend?"

### Links

Buttons that redirect users to specific pages.

### Spotify

Perfect for creators who share playlists, music, or podcasts.

### YouTube

Add:

- Channels
- Individual videos

### Maps

Direct users to your store, office, restaurant, or any physical location.

---

## Why triggers increase engagement

Triggers act as **entry points into the conversation**.

They help users discover that they can:

- Ask questions
- Explore your content
- Navigate information more easily

As a result, chat usage and engagement tend to increase significantly.

---

## Improving your knowledge sources

Another important factor in your chat's performance is **the quality of the knowledge sources you provide**.

Attlas responds based on the content you add.

This means that **better content leads to better answers**.

### What types of content work best?

Creators often achieve great results by adding:

- YouTube videos
- Guides and tutorials
- Pages with detailed explanations
- Tool and resource lists

Content that thoroughly explains a topic helps the chat provide more accurate and useful answers.

Keeping your knowledge base organized also helps your chat remain valuable over time.

---

## Customizing your chat's appearance

Finally, you can improve the user experience by adjusting **your chat's visual identity**.

This allows your chat to match the look and feel of your content and brand.

### Using the theme editor

The Theme Editor allows you to customize several visual elements.

You can adjust:

- Colors
- Typography
- Button styles
- Background images

These changes help transform your chat into something that feels like **a natural part of your brand**.

### Creating an experience that feels like you

When your chat's appearance matches your content, the experience feels much more cohesive.

Your Attlas stops feeling like just another tool.

Instead, it becomes **an extension of your online presence**.

---

## Conclusion

Creating a chat is only the first step.

The real power of Attlas emerges when you start refining:

- Personality
- Triggers
- Knowledge sources
- Visual customization

With these adjustments, your Attlas becomes much more than a chat.

It becomes **an interactive assistant for your audience**.
