As companies grow, three common problems begin to appear:

1. Support teams spend their time answering the same questions repeatedly.
2. Valuable information exists but customers struggle to find it.
3. Documentation becomes increasingly difficult to navigate.

Support teams deal with the same questions every day:

- What is the delivery time?
- How does the refund process work?
- How do I use the product?
- How do I solve a simple issue?

These questions are important, but they are not complex.

Yet they consume valuable time.

Time that could be spent:

- Solving critical cases
- Improving customer experience
- Building better products
- Supporting more strategic initiatives

In practice, many teams become trapped in a cycle of repetitive answers.
