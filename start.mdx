## Welcome to Attlas

Attlas allows you to transform your knowledge into intelligent conversations in just a few minutes.

This guide will walk you through the key steps to create your first chat, add content, customize its behavior and share it with your audience.

Inside this Getting Started section, you'll find everything you need to move from setup to launch, learning how to build a chat that understands your content and delivers accurate, contextual responses.

Follow each step and discover **how easy it is to create conversational experiences** powered by your own knowledge.
