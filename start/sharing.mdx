Once your chat has been configured and trained, it's time to share it with the world.

Attlas provides multiple distribution methods so you can choose the one that best fits your audience, channel, or project.

To access these options, click the **<kbd>Share</kbd>** button located in the top-right corner of the platform.

**\[SHARING SCREEN IMAGE\]**

---

## Sharing options

Attlas offers four main ways to share your chat:

- Direct link
- Scan & Chat (QR Code)
- Chat bubble
- Display on a page

Let's explore each option.

---

## 🔗 Direct link

The Direct link is the simplest and fastest way to share your chat.

When you create a chat, Attlas automatically generates a unique URL that can be shared anywhere.

You can use this link on:

- Social media
- WhatsApp
- Email
- Instagram bio
- LinkedIn
- Email signatures
- Promotional materials

Simply copy the link and share it with your audience.

---

## 📱 Scan & Chat

The **Scan & Chat** option automatically generates a QR Code for your chat.

This QR Code can be downloaded and used in both physical and digital materials.

**Examples of use:**

- Business cards
- Menus
- Flyers
- Presentations
- Catalogs
- Packaging
- Events and trade shows

When scanned, users will be taken directly to your chat.

**Note:** If you change your username in the **Profile** section, you will need to generate a new QR Code.

---

## 💬 Add a chat bubble

The **Add a chat bubble** option allows you to add a floating Attlas chat button to your website.

**\[CHAT BUBBLE SECTION IMAGE\]**

When enabled, Attlas will generate a small JavaScript code snippet.

Simply copy the code and add it to your website to display a chat bubble similar to those commonly used by customer support platforms.

When visitors click the bubble, your chat will open instantly.

This option is ideal for:

- Company websites
- Landing pages
- Online stores
- Blogs
- Corporate portals

<Tip>
  On most platforms, you only need to paste the code before the closing`</body>` tag.
</Tip>

---

## 🖥️ Display on a page

The **Display on a page** option allows you to embed the full chat directly into a webpage.

**\[DISPLAY ON A PAGE SECTION IMAGE\]**

Attlas automatically generates an embed code using an iframe.

By adding this code to your website, blog, or Notion page, the chat will appear directly within the page content.

This option is ideal for:

- Help centers
- Knowledge bases
- Documentation sites
- Support pages
- Internal portals
- Company websites

Unlike the Chat bubble option, the chat remains fully visible on the page, creating a more integrated user experience.

<Tip>
  You can adjust the iframe's width and height to fit your website layout.
</Tip>

---

## Which option should you choose?

Each sharing method serves a different purpose:

| Option | Best for |
| :-- | :-- |
| Direct link | Quick sharing across any channel |
| Scan & Chat | Physical materials and QR Codes |
| Chat bubble | Adding an assistant to your website |
| Display on a page | Embedding the full chat into a webpage |

You can use one or several of these options at the same time, creating more opportunities for users to interact with your chat.
