A well-designed chat doesn't just look better — it creates a more professional, memorable, and engaging experience for your users.

In the sections below, you'll learn how to customize every visual aspect of your Attlas chat.

---

## Themes

The **Themes** tab is Attlas visual customization space.

This is where you can adjust colors, typography, styles, and other visual elements to make your chat fully aligned with your identity.

Themes were designed to help you create an experience that truly feels like yours — more beautiful, more professional, and more engaging for everyone who interacts with your chat.

<Check>
  Go to `Settings → Themes` to personalize your chat's appearance.
</Check>

---

## What you'll find in the themes tab

Inside the Themes tab, you'll find the following sections:

- Avatar
- Background
- Buttons
- Chat Bubbles
- Typography
- Social Icons

Let's explore each one in detail.

<Info>
  **Live Preview:** Throughout the customization process, you'll be able to see all changes instantly in a real-time chat preview. This makes customization easier and ensures everything looks exactly the way you want before saving.
</Info>

---

## Avatar

In the **Avatar** section, you can control how your profile image is displayed.

### Image shape

Choose between:

- Square
- Rounded
- Circle

### Size

Select one of the available sizes:

- Small
- Medium
- Large

---

## Background

The **Background** section is one of the most powerful customization tools available in Themes.

You can customize your chat background in two different ways:

### Solid color

Choose a color that represents your brand, project, or personal preference.

### Custom image

Upload your own image, wallpaper, or any visual asset that represents your identity.

To add an image, click **Image** and upload the desired file. You can instantly preview and test different options directly within the chat preview.

<Tip>
  Attlas automatically adjusts text colors based on the selected background. Dark backgrounds use light text, while light backgrounds use dark text, ensuring optimal readability.
</Tip>

---

## Buttons

In the **Buttons** section, you can fully customize the appearance of your chat buttons.

### Shape

Choose between:

- Square
- Rounded
- Pill

### Style

Select the button style:

- Filled
- Outline

### Colors

Customize:

- Background color for filled buttons
- Arrow color displayed inside buttons

These options allow you to create buttons that are subtle, modern, or perfectly aligned with your visual identity.

---

## Chat bubbles

In the **Chat Bubbles** section, you can define the color of the message bubbles displayed throughout conversations.

This customization helps reinforce your visual identity and creates a more enjoyable experience for users.

<Info>
  Chat bubbles automatically follow the same shape style selected for your buttons, ensuring visual consistency across the entire chat.
</Info>

---

## Typography

In the **Typography** section, you can choose the fonts used throughout your chat.

### Fonts

You can customize:

- Chat title font
- Conversation font

### Font size

Choose between:

- Small
- Medium
- Large

### Link color

You can also customize the color of links displayed during conversations.

Whenever Attlas shares a link — such as a website, landing page, or external resource — it will use your selected color, keeping the experience visually consistent with your brand.

These settings improve readability and help adapt the chat to your preferred style.

---

## Social icons

In the **social icons** section, you can define the visual style of your social media icons.

Available styles include:

- **Minimal:** Clean and subtle icons.
- **Outline:** Icons with highlighted borders and outlines.
- **Colored:** Icons displayed using the original colors of each platform.

---

## Make it truly yours

Once you've finished configuring the Themes tab, your chat will reflect your visual identity much more accurately.

The content remains the same — but the visual experience becomes more professional, memorable, and aligned with your brand.

---

## Testing your chat

After saving your changes, all customizations will be applied automatically and your chat will be ready to share.

Before doing so, we recommend testing your chat using the **Playground**.

The Playground is a dedicated environment where you can experiment with your chat's knowledge, personality, and responses without consuming messages from your plan.

---

## Why customize your chat?

These settings allow Attlas to communicate exactly the way you want.

You can create a chat that is:

- More **professional** for businesses and organizations;
- More **casual** for creators and communities;
- More **welcoming** for education, support, or guidance;
- More **creative** for entertainment and interactive experiences.

All of this can be configured in a simple, intuitive, and flexible way, ensuring that your chat's personality reflects your brand and delivers a more consistent experience to users.
