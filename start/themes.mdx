Themes were designed to help you create an experience that truly feels like yours — more beautiful, more professional, and more engaging for everyone who interacts with your chat.

The Themes section allows you to quickly change your chat's appearance using pre-built themes.

<Check>
  Go to `Settings → Themes` to personalize your chat's appearance.
</Check>

![Themes](/images/themes.jpg)

## Choosing a theme

Attlas currently offers **42 ready-to-use themes**.

![Themes Attlas](/images/themes-attlas.jpg)

Each theme comes with its own combination of:

- Colors
- Visual styles
- Button designs

Simply select a theme and all visual changes will be applied automatically to your chat.

Feel free to explore different options until you find the one that best represents your brand, project, or visual identity.

## Light mode and dark mode

In addition to themes, you can choose how your chat is displayed.

### Light mode

Uses a clean and bright interface.

### Dark mode

Uses a dark interface and automatically adapts the theme colors for a better visual experience.

You can switch between modes at any time.

<Info>
  The **Customize** section, which allows you to personalize colors, typography, layout, and background images, is available exclusively on the **Attlas Starter** and **Attlas PRO** plans.
</Info>
